中文安裝指引:

1. 將 mod_captcha.php 放到 module/ 目錄
2. 找尋使用的字型檔 (預設為 arial.ttf) 並放置在 module/ 目錄，可自由添加不同的字型
3. 修改 mod_captcha.php，可自由修改 $this->CAPTCHA_ 前綴的參數
4. 修改 config.php，在模組載入區塊下加一行 $ModuleList[] = 'mod_captcha';

English Instruction:

1. Put "mod_captcha.php" into the module/ directory.
2. Search "arial.ttf" or other font files you like, and put them into the module/ directory, too.
3. Use your favorite editor to open "mod_captcha.php". You can change the "$this->CAPTCHA_" prefix settings.
4. Finally open "config.php" and add one line ($ModuleList[] = 'mod_showhide';) into the "Modules to be loaded" block.
